Jason's Coronavirus Page

What?

This is Jason's collection of information about CoronaVirus (COVID-19), which is (was?) a global pandemic in 2020. For Jason, life during the pandemic often feels overwhelming due to the rapid and often confusing pace of information regarding the virus. Jason finds comfort in categorizing the information he's found, thus, this page exists.

Disclaimer

Jason is not a trained scientist or doctor. This page is not an authority on anything, it's simply a collection of information that Jason hopes you might find useful. Do your own research. Listen to the WHO, the CDC, and be safe.
